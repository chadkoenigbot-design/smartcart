Everything SmartCart Can Do

Profile & Saved Lists

Capture your household size, ZIP, membership, and preferences once — SmartCart remembers it all locally, along with saved grocery lists and planner history.

Fitness Goal Planner

Describe any goal — cut calories, bulk up, meal prep, balance macros — and SmartCart will build a grounded grocery list with recipes, snacks, and iterative refinements.

Price Optimizer

Send any list to the optimizer to see exactly what belongs in the warehouse cart vs. the local store. Built-in logic for perishability, trip value, and membership ROI.
